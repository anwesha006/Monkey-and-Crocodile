Once upon a time in a forest there was a monkey who resided on a jamun tree which was on the banks of a river. 
In the same forest there was a crocodile and his wife.
One day crocodile came to the banks of the river and rested under the tree the monkey offered him sone fruits.
The crocodile came back the next day for more fruits as he loved them.
As days passed by the crocodile and the monkey became good friends.
